For the final project, I worked independently to analyze the risks associated with Pampered Petsâ€™ expanded digital transformation. This included incorporating an international supply chain, automated warehouses, and addressing concerns about quality and supply chain security raised by high-profile clients.

What I Did:

  3. Recommendations and Strategies: I proposed solutions such as implementing a Vendor Management System to enhance supplier reliability, predictive logistics tools to minimize transportation delays, and a robust disaster recovery strategy to ensure business continuity
